Chicken Piccata

Description

Here I will teach you how to create Chicken Piccata from scratch. This has been a family tradition in the George household for years and now I am ofering you a seat at table. Hope you enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 2 large skinless, boneless chicken breats
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 stick butter
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 1/2 cup white wine
  • 3 medium lemons, juiced
  • 2 tablespoons drained capers
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley

  1. Slice chicken breats horizontally to yield 4 pices. place breasts between 2 sheets of plastic wrap and pound to 1/4-inch thin. Place flour on a plate and dredge each breast on both sides in flour.
  2. Melt butter in a large skillet over medium heat. Put chicken into the skillet and season tops with salt and pepper. Turn when nicely browned, about 4 minutes, and season again. Cook second side until browned, about 4 minutes. Transfer breasts to a plate, reserving skillet dripppings.
  3. Add wine and lemon juice to the skillet, stirring to scrape up any browned bits. Simmer to reduce and thicken sauce, about 5 minutes more. Add capers and pour sauce over chicken breasts on the plate. Sprinkle with parsley.
